# Reports

This folder contains presentations and supporting documentation related to GIS and remote sensing analyses of environmental change in the Aral Sea region of Kazakhstan.

## Contents

* **Aral Coastline.pptx** – Presentation examining coastline change in the North Aral Sea using remote sensing and GIS techniques.

* **Desertification in the Aral Sea Region.pdf** – Report investigating land-cover patterns and environmental change associated with desertification processes in the Aral Sea region.
